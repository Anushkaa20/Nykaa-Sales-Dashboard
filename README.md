# Nykaa-Sales-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard analyzing Nykaa's beauty e-commerce performance across products, customers, discounts, and profitability.

# Nykaa Sales Dashboard — Power BI

A multi-page interactive business intelligence dashboard built in Power BI
to analyze sales, customer behavior, product performance, and profitability
for Nykaa — India's leading beauty e-commerce platform.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Home
Branded landing page with navigation to all report sections.

### 2. Overview
High-level KPIs at a glance:
- Total Sales: 10M
- Total Orders: 2,000
- Total Quantity: 9,925
- Average Order Value: 5.15K
- Profit: 8.23M
- Sales by Category (bar chart)
- Payment Mode Distribution (donut chart)
- Monthly Sales Trend (line chart)

### 3. Product Analysis
- Top 10 Products by Sales and Quantity
- Sales by Brand (Huda Beauty leads at 4.5M)
- Category vs Sub-Category breakdown (Eye Makeup tops at 5.4M)
- Filters by Brand and Category

### 4. Customer Analysis
- Orders by Channel (Website: 692, Online App: 663, Offline Store: 645)
- Total Sales by Gender (Male 52.71% vs Female 47.29%)
- Regional Sales Map (East, West, North, South)
- Filter by Region and Brand

### 5. Discount and Profit Analysis
- Total Discount: 2.06M
- Profit: 8.23M
- Total Sales by Sale Event:
  - Summer Sale: 2.68M
  - Hot Pink Sale: 2.58M
  - Winter Sale: 2.53M
  - Flash Sale: 2.50M

## Data Model
The dataset includes the following tables:
`Brands` | `Categories` | `Customers` | `Date` | `Order_Items` | `Orders` | `Products`

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- DAX for calculated measures
- Power Query for data transformation

## Key Insights
- Eye Makeup is the highest-grossing category at 5.4M
- Huda Beauty dominates brand-wise sales
- Sales are nearly flat month-over-month, suggesting stable demand
- All four sales channels and regions contribute roughly equally
- Summer Sale drives the highest revenue among promotional events
